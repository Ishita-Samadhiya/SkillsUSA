FAQ

When is the festival?

The festival takes place on the last full weekend of September each year, starting on Wednesday night and concluding on Sunday.

Where is the festival located?

The festival is held in a large park in Central Valley, CA. A detailed map of the festival layout is available on our website.

How can I purchase carnival tickets?

Carnival tickets go on sale a week before the event and are available at the local Chamber of Commerce office or participating Circle K stores.

What does a ticket coupon get me?

Each $30 ticket coupon can be exchanged for a booklet of 30 individual ride tickets or for an unlimited ride wristband on Thursdays and Sundays.

How can I participate in the parade?

To enter the parade, please contact Isabel at isabel@dinubachamber.com. All entries must be registered a week prior to the event.

How do I become a food or craft vendor?

For information on becoming a vendor, please email Isabel at isabel@dinubachamber.com. Vendor applications and guidelines are available on our website.

Is there accessible parking available?

Yes, accessible parking is available near the alleyway food vendor side entrance. Please be aware of vendor and permitted parking areas to avoid towing.

Are there any road closures I should be aware of?

Yes, some roads around the festival area will be closed for the event. We advise checking our website for the latest information on road closures.

What safety measures are in place at the festival?

We prioritize the safety of all attendees with security personnel on site, first aid stations, and emergency contact information readily available.

Is the festival accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, the festival is accessible, with designated parking, accessible restrooms, and accommodations for individuals with disabilities.

Are pets allowed at the festival?

For the safety and comfort of all attendees, pets are not allowed, with the exception of service animals.

What happens in case of bad weather?

The festival is a rain or shine event. We recommend checking the weather forecast and dressing appropriately.
